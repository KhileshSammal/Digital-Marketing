Case study - AuditMyAds™

The Problem

📝

Legacy structure and bid strategy

Adgroups were not correctly segmented and the account was using manual CPC.

📝

Incorrect use of Responsive Search Ads

Ad content wasn’t diverse, with no focus on benefits and no call to action.

📝

Issues with search terms data

Ads were wasting budget with clicks for business names which didn’t convert and were difficult to eliminate with negatives on an ongoing basis.

The Solution

✅

Full campaign rebuild

Detailed keyword research to ensure full coverage, campaign restructured in a way that is well segmented, but still allowed for smart bidding success.

✅

Ad Content Research

We listed all benefits, USPs and customer actions to write relevant and compelling ads.

✅

Use of AI

Using an engineered prompt, I used ChatGPT to identify search terms (within large 9k+ search term reports) containing the names of other businesses in order to eliminate them.
